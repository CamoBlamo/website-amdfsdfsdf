Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 20, 2026

We use data to provide and improve DevDock, authenticate users, maintain security, process support requests, and communicate important service updates.

When you sign in, we create secure session tokens and cookies so you can access your account. Session information is used only for authentication and platform security.

4. Incident and Service Data

Service reliability details may be displayed through our public status tooling. Incident data is used to keep users informed about availability and platform health.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share limited information with trusted service providers required to operate DevDock (for example, hosting, authentication, and monitoring services).

6. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary for business, legal, and security purposes. You may request deletion of your account data by contacting support.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or export your data. Contact us to submit a request and we will respond in a reasonable timeframe.

8. Contact

For privacy-related requests, contact us through our contact page.
