DevDock Application Information

DevDock is a platform for managing development teams, providing important tools to optimize collaboration and productivity.

DevDock has multiple positions available for interested candidates. Those include:

  • Customer Support
  • Public Relations
  • Beta Testers (Closed Currently)

To apply for any of these positions, please find your desired role and click the apply button. We look forward to hearing from you!

Customer Support

As a Customer Support representative, you will be responsible for assisting our users with any issues they may encounter while using DevDock. This includes troubleshooting technical problems, providing guidance on how to use the platform, and ensuring a positive user experience.

Public Relations

As a Public Relations specialist, you will be responsible for managing the public image of DevDock. This includes creating and distributing press releases, managing social media accounts, and building relationships with media outlets to promote our platform.

Beta Testers

As a Beta Tester, you will have the opportunity to test new features and updates before they are released to the public. This role is currently closed, but we encourage you to check back for future opportunities.
